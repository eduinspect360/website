How it works

From notification to improvement plan — six steps.

The complete inspection journey, managed end-to-end in EDUINSPECT360.

1

Pre-inspection setup

Framework selected, inspector assigned, school notified — all automated from a single action in the platform.

2

Pre-inspection planning

Inspector reviews school data history. School prepares evidence in the platform using structured templates aligned to the EIF.

3

On-site inspection

Evidence captured in real time via mobile or tablet. All observations, grades, and documents stored centrally and shared with the lead inspector immediately.

4

Moderation

Lead inspector reviews evidence, agrees grades, adds commentary. All changes tracked with timestamps for full audit trail.

5

Automated report generation

Draft report auto-generated from structured data. Reviewed, approved, and published — without reformatting or rewriting.

6

Post-inspection improvement tracking

School improvement actions logged and tracked against the Ofsted evaluation areas over time. Progress visible to both the school and the authority.
