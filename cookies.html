Data Governance

Transparency is at the core of our educational mission. This document outlines how EDUINSPECT360 handles data for Schools, Local Authorities or international equivalent, Multi-Academy Trusts and Government Agencies.

1. What are Cookies? 2. How we use them

1. Our Use of Cookies

EDUINSPECT360 Limited uses cookies and similar technologies to ensure our inspection platform remains secure, performs efficiently, and provides a seamless user experience. This policy explains what these technologies are and your rights to control them.

2. Classification of Cookies

We categorize our cookies into the following groups based on UK GDPR and PECR guidelines:

Category Purpose
Strictly Necessary Essential for site security and form submissions (e.g., Formspree anti-spam tokens).
Performance Anonymous data that helps us understand which platform features are most used by inspectors.
Functionality Used to remember your preferences and regional settings for the inspection framework.

3. Managing Your Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time via our consent banner. Additionally, most web browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings. Please note that disabling strictly necessary cookies may prevent the contact form or platform dashboard from functioning correctly.
