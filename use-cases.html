Local authorities

For Directors of Education and school improvement teams.

Local authorities face a unique challenge: oversight responsibility for schools they do not control, with reducing resources and increasing accountability expectations from central government, boards, and elected members.

The challenge

Fragmented inspection data across dozens of schools. No single view of performance. Manual reporting to scrutiny committees. Rising costs of a deployed inspector workforce with no digital coordination tools.

How EDUINSPECT360 helps

Real-time portfolio dashboard. Automated inspection scheduling and reporting. Evidence coordination across your school improvement team. Board-ready data exports in one click.

Fewer admin hours. More time in schools.

Key capabilities for local authorities

Authority-wide portfolio view

All schools, all inspection statuses, all performance data — in one dashboard updated in real time.

Inspector workforce management

Schedule, allocate, and track inspector time. Automatically match inspectors to schools by specialism and availability.

Scrutiny-ready reporting

Automated summary reports for board meetings, DfE returns, and elected member briefings — configurable by audience.

LA QA visit framework

Separate template library for advisory visits and governor support — distinct from formal Ofsted inspections but in the same system.

Multi-Academy Trusts

For CEO and executive principal teams.

MATs are accountable for educational outcomes across a growing portfolio of schools. The expectation from the Regional Director and the Trust Board is clear: visibility, consistency, and continuous improvement at scale.

The MAT challenge

Individual school performance data sits in isolation. Inconsistent inspection approaches across the trust. Executive team blind spots between formal inspections. Difficulty evidencing quality at trust board level.

How EDUINSPECT360 helps

Trust-wide dashboard showing all schools, inspection cycle status, and performance trajectories. Standardised inspection frameworks across the trust. Executive reporting packs generated automatically.

Consistent quality assurance, every school
International & other bodies

Beyond the UK — configurable for any inspection framework.

EDUINSPECT360 is built on a flexible, configurable framework engine. It is not locked to the UK Ofsted model — any structured inspection framework can be configured, making it suitable for international education ministries, accreditation bodies, and regional inspection authorities worldwide.

Education ministries

Configure national inspection frameworks with custom grade descriptors, language settings, and reporting formats aligned to local regulatory requirements.

Accreditation bodies

Structured evidence collection against accreditation criteria. Multi-school or multi-campus views. Exportable evidence packs for accreditation panel review.

Regional inspection services

Multi-authority or multi-region deployments with central oversight. Role-based access separating regional and national teams with configurable data sharing.
