Data Governance

Our commitment to UK and Global school inspection standards. We ensure your organisation's data is handled with rigorous security and ethical oversight.

1. Security Overview 2. Data Residency 3. AI & Ethics

1. Security Overview

We implement "Privacy by Design" across the EDUINSPECT360 platform, using enterprise-grade encryption (AES-256) for all school inspection data.

2. Data Residency

All data is hosted within ISO 27001 certified data centers in the United Kingdom (or in country, where applicable), ensuring compliance with UK GDPR residency requirements (or where applicable GDPR compliance for the region specified at contract).

3. AI & Ethics

Human-in-the-Loop Principle:

Our platform uses AI to identify trends and draft report narratives. However, EDUINSPECT360 does not perform automated grading. All inspection findings must be reviewed and validated by a human lead inspector.
