Data Governance & Security

EDUINSPECT360 is built on a foundation of "Privacy by Design," ensuring that UK education data is handled with the highest levels of integrity.

AI & Ethics Statement

As we integrate intelligent automation to assist with the inspection process, we adhere to the following principles:

  • • No Automated Grading: The platform provides data-driven evidence, but final inspection judgements are always made by professional inspectors.
  • • Algorithmic Transparency: We provide clear "Explainability" for any auto-suggested narrative or risk-flags within the dashboard.
  • • GDPR Compliance: Our use of AI is fully compliant with UK GDPR requirements regarding profiling and automated processing.

Data Residency

All data is hosted in ISO 27001 certified data centres located within the United Kingdom. We do not transfer personal data outside of the EEA without explicit contractual safeguards.
